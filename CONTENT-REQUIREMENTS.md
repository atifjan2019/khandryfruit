# Client content required

- Full legal business identity and approved German legal texts.
- VAT status/rates and invoice requirements from a qualified adviser.
- Product list, SKUs, approved prices, compare-at/cost prices and verified inventory.
- Ingredients, allergens/cross-contamination, nutrition per 100 g, net weight, storage, shelf life, country/region, responsible food business, lot/BBD workflow.
- Product photography with usage rights and translated alt text.
- Evidence/certificates for every product claim.
- Shipping carrier contract, zones, rates, weights, free threshold, dispatch/delivery wording and return handling.
- Verified sending domain and transactional email copy approval.
- Final German and English story, sourcing, recipe, wholesale, FAQ and support content.
- Domain, analytics identifiers and consent/legal approval.
